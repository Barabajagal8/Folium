    Frequently Asked Questions !

    What is Folium?

    +

    Folium is a social networking gardening app for all who enjoy gardening. From amateurs to professionals, all who have an interest in gardening or plants are welcome.

    Do I have to pay to become a member?

    +

    No, you do not have to become a member. However, to be able to enjoy all the features of our website it will probably be in your best interest to create a free account, and then you can engage with people who share your interest! The passion to Garden!

    My region is blocked, is there still a way to become a member?

    +

    Unfortunately, no. If your region is blocked there is little we can do to suggest on how you can become a member. There is hope though, we are avidly working to expand the regions our website is available so that all can be included.

    How do I make a free account.

    +

    If you do not already have a free account then simply click the sign-up button at the top of the page. Follow the steps and you will be well on your way to joining a exciting community of gardeners.

    Why do I have to use your website?

    +

    You do not have to use our website. There is absolutely no pressure to do so. But, if you like plants or if you like to garden, then maybe we have what you're looking for. Step through the portal and find out!
